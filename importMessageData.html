Importing Message Data with AI

OVERVIEW

Problem. Title agencies lose time reviewing info across multiple channels such as email, phone, fax, and portal.

Constraints. Our approach was impacted by the capabilities and accuracy of our AI at the time.

My Role. I led design alongside an engineer and 2 product managers.

Result. Our solution uses AI to identify, compare, and extract relevant data from messages received by Qualia users.

View Details

Solution

In June 2024, our team released an AI messaging tool that reduced the need for manual entry by doing the following:

  1. Identify pertinent closing data within a message
  2. Compare new data to existing Qualia file data
  3. Import data directly from a message into a Qualia file

Launch & Findings

We started with a beta of select clients. After achieving an accuracy rate >70% and seeing usage metrics stabalize, we moved forward with a wide launch.

  • ~34% click rate. This means around 34% of all identified fields are engages with (i.e. hovered over or clicked).
  • ~22% of suggestions accepted. More research is needed to understand whether higher conversion rates are being impacted by user trust, different role types, confusion, or something else.
  • <1% of suggestions dismissed. Users rarely outright reject a suggestion.

In the short term, we plan to add support for additional fields that customers have indicated are important. In parallel, we plan to dig into the conditions affecting conversion rate and what levers we can pull to see improvement.
